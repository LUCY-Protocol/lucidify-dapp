About Lucidify

Designed for clarity, built for durability.

LUCIDIFY (LUCY) is a deflationary Polygon-based asset focused on transparent mechanics, risk-managed launches, and long-term ecosystem value. By combining auto-burn, dynamic tax, and anti-whale limits, LUCY is engineered to create sustainable on-chain liquidity and a disciplined growth trajectory.

Clear tokenomics

No hidden mints. No surprise supply shocks. LUCY is a fixed-supply token with clearly defined allocations and fully trackable flows to liquidity, marketing, and ecosystem development.

Deploy with control

Trading can be locked or enabled by the owner, with blacklist controls and configurable max wallet / max transaction limits to mitigate early manipulation and bot-driven volatility.
